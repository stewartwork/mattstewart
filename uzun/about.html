Firm Overview

Founded in 1993 by principals Tamer Uzun, James Case, Martin Cuadra and Larry McDowell, Uzun+Case is one of the largest structural engineering firms in the Southeastern US. Our growth has been fueled by our technical expertise, creative design approach, and teamwork orientation. We have an extensive portfolio of commercial, institutional, educational, and healthcare projects. Having completed developments in excess of 2 million square feet, we are capable of handling challenging projects of any size.

What We Do

In order to provide clients with solutions that are imaginative, technically sound, and economical, Uzun+Case continually strives to be at the leading edge of structural engineering practice. Our engineers have advanced degrees from leading universities and have an average of 10+ years of professional experience. Our senior principals each have 30+ years of experience.

Working With Us During Design

We believe that design is integration. We judge our solutions by how well the structural needs of the project are integrated with its architectural, construction, and budgetary requirements.

In order to achieve this, principals personally guide the conceptual design of a project. They put forth and test multiple proposals in order to arrive at the most appropriate structural solutions.

Working With Us During Construction

We see the construction phase of a project as an extension of its design phase. It is our policy for the design engineer to stay with a project through its construction. This ensures continuity of design intent and exceptional construction phase service.

We take pride in providing timely responses to questions, quickly reviewing shop drawings, and responding creatively to field problems.
